Dr. Jane Smith

Associate Professor of Computational Biology

Department of Biology · University of Science

I am a computational biologist working at the intersection of machine learning and genomics. My research develops algorithms for decoding gene regulatory networks and understanding their role in disease. Previously I was a postdoctoral fellow at the Broad Institute, MIT & Harvard.
