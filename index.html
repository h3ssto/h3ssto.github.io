Tobias Heß

Academic Staff

DHBW Heidenheim

After graduating with a master’s degree in mathematics from the University of Ulm, Germany, I am currently pursuing a PhD in computer science, also at the University of Ulm. My reasearch focus is the analysis of configurable (software) systems, typically by means of knowledge compilation, especially binary decision diagrams.

I currently work as a lecturer and system administrator at the Baden-Wuerttemberg Cooperative State University (DHBW) Heidenheim. Therein, my core responsibility is the creation of interdisciplinary concepts and systems spanning multiple study programs.
